ABOUT

"Shoesociety is a website engaged in information about various kinds of shoes in the world. Shoesociety was founded in early 2022 which was made by students at a university in Bandung. The name Shoesociety is a combination of the words "Shoes" which means shoes and "Society" which means society. Then the two words are combined to become "SHOESOCIETY" which means "various kinds of shoes and brands used by people in the world"
